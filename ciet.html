ABOUT CIET

The Coimbatore Institute of Engineering and Technology (CIET) was founded in 2001 by the Kovai Kalaimagal Educational Trust (KKET) aims to provide a broad spectrum of academic options to ambitious young individuals.

Situated approximately 25 kilometers from the city of Coimbatore, the institute is set in a scenic and tranquil 26.5-acre campus, surrounded by lush green hillocks, creating a peaceful learning environment.
